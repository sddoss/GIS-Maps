National Register of Historic Places

Lexington, KY

The National Register of Historic Places map list all the historic places in Lexington, It also highlights all the historic districts and parks in round the city of Lexington.

The map has information embeded in the map so if you click on the different highlighted polygons in the map it will give you information about that historic place.

The information embeded in the map inculded the name of the historic place, period of significance, historic significance, and the NRHP refences number. The NRHP refences

number can be used to find more information about the place from the National Register of Historic Place website. The links to the NRHP website is the secound hyperlink beelow

below the map.
