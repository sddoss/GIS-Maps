National Register of Historic Places Lexington,KY Map

National Register of Historic Places Datebase

The National Register of Historic Places map list all the historic places in Lexington, It also highlights all the historic districts and parks in round the city of Lexington.

The map has information embedded in the map so if you click on the different highlighted polygons in the map it will give you information about that historic place.

The information embedded in the map included the name of the historic place, period of significance, historic significance, and the NRHP reference number. The NRHP reference

number can be used to find more information about the place from the National Register of Historic Place website. The links to the NRHP website is the second hyperlink below

below the map.
